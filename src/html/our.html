Our Story

Born from a love of coffee

Kopi Bara started as a small weekend cart in 2018 — just a handgrinder, a pour-over kit, and a dream. Today we're a neighborhood staple, roasting our own beans and serving hundreds of happycustomers every day.

We partner with Cambodian farmers to source the finest single-originbeans, roasted in-house every week to guarantee the freshest cup in the city.
